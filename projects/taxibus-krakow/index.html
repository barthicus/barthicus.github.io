TaxiBus Kraków

Description

TaxiBus Kraków is the Polish company with a long history that needed full rework of their old website with a possibility to manage services from administration panel.

The new portal main goal was to offer better booking turs and travels experience and be multilingual (Polish and English).

The new CMS panel allows company employers to** add new offer on the website** and set their prices in both currencies. One of the most important portal sections was interactive booking form where customer can calculate prices of the custom tour/travel and book it instantly.

Technologies

Website was created using Wordpress and every user interactions were handled by JavaScript (mostly jQuery) using Grunt as bundler to minify/concatenate code and compile LESS stylesheets.

To allow TaxiBus Kraków to manage their offer from administration panel a custom plugin has been written (in PHP). The client can add new services, edit their prices and descriptions in both languages and to do other general CMS based actions (such as edit pages content).
